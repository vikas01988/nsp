Poornima Transport Management System




A student can park his/her personal vehicle in the available parking areas on campus. A student needs to register his/her vehicle details. On completion of registration, a parking lot along with a parking slot will be assigned to that student.


A student can make use of the bus facility provided by the college. A student needs to register for a route provided by the college. On completion of registration a bus number along with the seat number will be assigned to that student.
