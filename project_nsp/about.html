Transport Management System

The transport management system is an initiative to ease the process of vehicle management within the RVCE campus. Its primary motive is to simplify the process of registering for the college bus and personal vehicle parking facilities. Gone are the days when students and staff were required to stand in long queues, fill up numerous forms and personally submit it to the institution! With the introduction of the transport management portal, you can now obtain a bus pass or a vehicle parking pass in a matter of minutes!

This portal also helps to maintain attendance records of members travelling by college buses. Vehicles entering and leaving the campus are also monitored with the help of this system. Thus, this portal goes a long way in improving the transport management in the institution.
