RVCE Transport Management System




Every vehicle registration done by a student is given a unique vehicle ID. Attendance is taken for every parking area available. On generating the vehicle attendance sheet, you will be able to mark if a vehicle is present in the alloted parking area or not


Every student who enrolls for a college bus, a seat number is alloted. Attendance is taken for every bus seperately. On generating the bus attendance sheet, you will be able to take attendance of a student associated with that bus.
